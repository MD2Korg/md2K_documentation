MD2K Smoking and Eating Cessation
====================

## Description
This study evaluates the feasibility of a just-in-time intervention to delay or prevent smoking relapse in smokers attempting to quit.

```{admonition} Study Details
**Users:** 225

**Person-Days:** 3,150

**Samples:** 136 billion

**Sensors:** AutoSense, MotionSense, Phone
```
